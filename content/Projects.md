# Projects

A short list of things I’ve built or contributed to. If something here interests you, I’m happy to walk through the architecture and tradeoffs.

## [idx-bei](https://github.com/nichsedge/idx-bei)

Indonesia Stock Exchange (IDX/BEI) data toolkit: scraping + processing + analysis (incl. optional Neo4j ingestion) for market and company fundamentals.

## [portfolio-integration](https://github.com/nichsedge/portfolio-integration)

Python/Node monorepo that integrates portfolio data from KSEI, Binance, DeBank, Hyperliquid, and manual CSV into unified daily snapshots.

## [social-media-analytics](https://github.com/nichsedge/social-media-analytics)

End-to-end social media analytics course repo: data collection (API/Twint), text mining, topic modeling, stance detection, and network analysis (PageRank).

## [k8s-ansible-gcp](https://github.com/nichsedge/k8s-ansible-gcp)

Infrastructure management on GCP using Kubernetes + Ansible automation (cluster provisioning and ops patterns).

## [pcomp](https://github.com/nichsedge/pcomp)

Scrapes Indonesian PC hardware prices from EnterKomputer and Viraindo for tracking and analysis.

## [indoscraping](https://github.com/nichsedge/indoscraping)

Collection of web scrapers for Indonesian news portals (Detik, Kompas, CNBC, CNN) and retail sites (Alfagift, Indomaret) in Python and Node.js.
