- **[[Dumb Ideas That Only Smart People Believe]]**  
  Certain complex ideas that seem clever but are actually misguided or overthought.

- **[[Arrival Fallacy]]**  
  When you think achieving a goal will satisfy you, but it turns out you never really knew what you wanted.

- **[[Sunk Cost Fallacy]]**  
  Persisting with something because you've already invested in it, even if it's no longer worth it.

- **[[Pygmalion Effect]]**  
  High expectations lead to improved performance.

- **[[Placebo Effect]]**  
  A benefit from believing in a treatment, not the treatment itself (e.g., [[Dr. Frost]]’s dog).

- **[[Diderot Effect]]**  
  Getting one new thing causes a spiral of consumption to match the new standard (e.g., positive attitude, Victor Frankl).

- **[[Law of Reversed Effort]]**  
  Trying too hard often makes success less likely.

- **[[Barnum Effect]]**  
  Believing vague, general statements (like horoscopes or MBTI) are uniquely accurate.

- **[[Commitment Bias]]**  
  The tendency to remain committed to past decisions even when they no longer make sense.

- **[[Jonah Complex]]**  
  Fear of success or realizing one's full potential due to the responsibilities or changes it might bring.
