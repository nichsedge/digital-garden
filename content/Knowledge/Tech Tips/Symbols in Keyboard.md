List of all the **symbols** commonly found on a **standard English (US) QWERTY keyboard**, along with their **names** in English:

---

### 🔠 Top Row (Numbers & Symbols with Shift):

|Key|Symbol|Symbol Name|
|---|---|---|
|1|!|Exclamation mark|
|2|@|At sign|
|3|#|Hash / Pound sign|
|4|$|Dollar sign|
|5|%|Percent sign|
|6|^|Caret|
|7|&|Ampersand|
|8|*|Asterisk / Star|
|9|(|Left parenthesis|
|0|)|Right parenthesis|

---

### 🔡 Letters Row (with Shift):

|Key|Symbol|Symbol Name|
|---|---|---|
|-|_|Underscore|
|=|+|Plus sign|

---

### 🔤 Right Side (with Shift):

|Key|Symbol|Symbol Name|
|---|---|---|
|[|{|Left curly brace|
|]|}|Right curly brace|
|\|\ \||Backslash / Pipe|
|;|:|Colon|
|'|"|Quotation mark|
|,|<|Less than sign|
|.|>|Greater than sign|
|/|?|Question mark|

---

### 🔘 Other Important Keys:

|Key|Symbol/Function|Name|
|---|---|---|
|` (beside 1)|~ (with Shift)|Tilde|
|Tab|→ (indent)|Tab key|
|Caps Lock|A → a|Caps Lock|
|Shift||Shift key|
|Enter/Return|↵|Enter / Return key|
|Spacebar|(space)|Spacebar|
|Backspace|← (delete)|Backspace|
